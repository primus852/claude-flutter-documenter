## What is this app?

clean_flutter is a simple Flutter application with a single screen. When you open it, you see a counter starting at zero. Each time you tap the **Increment** button — the round plus-icon button in the bottom-right corner — the counter goes up by one. It is a straightforward demonstration of how a Flutter app responds to user input.

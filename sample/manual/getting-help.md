# Getting Help

If you have looked through this manual and still cannot find what you need, the support team is happy to help.

- **Email:** support@example.com
- **Help centre:** https://help.example.com
- **Phone:** +1 000 000 0000 (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm)

When you get in touch, it helps to describe:

1. What you were trying to do.
2. What you saw on screen.
3. The steps you had already tried.

This lets the support team get back to you with a solution as quickly as possible.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I can't sign in | Make sure your email and password are correct. If you've forgotten your password, use the Forgot password? link on the Login screen. |
| The page looks blank | Try refreshing the page (press F5 or Ctrl+R). If the problem persists, clear your browser cache. |
| My changes didn't save | Check that you clicked the Save button. Look for a confirmation message at the top of the screen. |
| The app is slow | Check your internet connection. Close other browser tabs to free up memory. |
| I can't find a feature | Use the search function (if available) or check the Table of Contents above. |

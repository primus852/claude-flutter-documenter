# Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I can't sign in | Make sure your email and password are correct. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot password?" option on the Login screen. |
| The screen looks blank | Close and reopen the app. If the problem persists, check your internet connection. |
| My changes didn't save | Check that you tapped the Save button. Look for a confirmation message on screen. |
| The app is slow | Check your internet connection. Try closing and restarting the app. |
| I can't find a feature | Check the [manual index](index.md) or search within the app. |

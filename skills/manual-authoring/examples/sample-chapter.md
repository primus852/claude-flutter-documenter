# Example: Authentication Chapter

This sample shows the expected output for an "Authentication" chapter group
covering Login and Forgot Password screens.

---

## Authentication

This section explains how to sign in to your account and recover access if you forget your password.

### Log In

Use the Login screen to access your account.

![Log In](screenshots/web/login.png)

**To sign in:**

1. Go to the **Log In** screen (open the app in your browser).
2. Type your email address in the **Email** field.
3. Type your password in the **Password** field.
4. Click **Sign In**.

You will arrive at your Dashboard after a successful sign-in.

> **Tip:** If you stay on a shared computer, uncheck **Remember me** so your session ends when you close the browser.

**Fields on this screen:**

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| Email | The email address you used when you created your account |
| Password | Your account password |

---

### Forgot Password

Use this screen to receive a password-reset link by email.

![Forgot Password](screenshots/web/forgot-password.png)

**To reset your password:**

1. On the **Log In** screen, click **Forgot password?**
2. Type your email address in the **Email** field.
3. Click **Send reset link**.
4. Check your email for a message from the app.
5. Click the link in the email.
6. Type a new password and click **Save password**.

> **Note:** The reset link expires after 30 minutes. If it has expired, repeat these steps to get a new one.
